Places Must Visit

Oasis of the Sea (Cruise)

I had visited in July 15 2022

It is a nice cruise ship that has many activites to do such as: riding a zipling, going to the many water slides, and walking down its many neighborhoods and streets. It has a fancy dining room which me and my family went to every night. You can go to its mall, theater, and ice skating rink where they do performances. There are a lot of things you can do on the Oasis of the Seas.



Ocean City

I had visited a LOT of times

Ocean City is a very special place. Not because of its beachs and activites but the fact that my grandparents own and take care of a beach house there for many years. We have many classic traditions that we do when we go there like: getting ice cream from Brusters, Crabbing, and visiting the boardwalk. Ocean City is a spectacular place to visit



Han Palace

I had visited a decent amount of times

Han Palace is a chinese restrarant that serves dim sum. One of my favorite dishes there is the crispy pork which is super crunchy. I also like the dumplings there too. Usually we go there on the weekends, maybe after a event or maybe we just want some good dim sum. The restruant is very popular so you should probably get reservations when going there.
